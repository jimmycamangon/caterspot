How to Apply

Welcome to the application process for joining our exclusive catering network. Please follow the steps below to ensure your application is completed accurately and efficiently.

1. Application Submission

To apply for listing your catering business on our platform, please submit the following information:

  • Business Name: The name of your catering business.
  • Owner: The name of the business owner or contact person.
  • Contact Number: A phone number where we can reach you.
  • Gmail: Your primary email address for correspondence.
  • Location: The physical address of your business.
  • Business Permit: A copy of your valid business permit.
  • Menu: A detailed menu of the catering services you offer.
  • Images: High-quality images showcasing your catering services.

2. Application Review

Upon receipt of your application, our admin team will review the submitted information. You will receive an email notification confirming the receipt of your application.

Processing Time: Please allow 2-3 business days for our team to review your application.

3. Approval and Account Setup

After reviewing your application, if approved, our team will contact you via the provided Gmail address. We will discuss the details and finalize the agreement between our platform and your business.

Account Creation: Once both parties are confident with the agreement, we will create an account for your business on our platform.

4. Credentials

Following the successful setup of your account, you will receive the following credentials via email:

  • Client Name: Your designated client name for your account.
  • Email: The email address designated for login.
  • Temporary Password: A temporary password for your initial login.

5. Accessing Your Account

To log in to your account, please visit our login page: caterspot.online/client. Use the provided credentials to access and manage your business profile.

If you have any questions or need further assistance, please do not hesitate to reach out to us at lucaterspot@gmail.com.

Thank you for choosing to be a part of our catering network. We look forward to a successful partnership!
